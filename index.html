Trumpet 101
Playing the Trumpet

Here's our guide to playing the trumpet. It is suited best for those beginning to play. This guide will get you started on the basic principles of trumpet playing.

Playing the trumpet is exciting to learn! Remember you are just starting out so your muscles will get tired. It's better to practice a little every day than a lot once or twice a week. Remember to rest when you feel your chops tired. As a general rule, rest twice as much as you play. Avoid excessive mouthpiece pressure, although some is needed for proper tone and to avoid air leaking under the mouthpiece.
